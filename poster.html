The Infographic Poster

Every year, the goal of the Southern Nevada Water Crisis team is to promote awareness of the crisis and steps to preserve/conserve water whenever we can. This inforgraphic poster will be used as our promotional poster/advertisement to every exhibit, meetings, club engagements, etc. that we are invited to speak and present. Made by our team, we'll post the current and previous inforgraphics created for historical purposes.
