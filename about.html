About Us

Welcome to the Southern Nevada Water Crisis project website, an online tool to help save water in your community. Improving water conservation and efficiency are the most efficient and cost-effective ways to quickly reduce water demand and extend supplies, providing flexibility for all Southern Nevada communities. Every drop of water saved and every suspected leak fixed will help secure our water supply.

This website provides you historical water information in Southern Nevada through satellite imagery, an infographic poster to give you knowledge about the current situation, and allows you to easily report leaks or water waste from your smartphone, tablet, laptop, or desktop computer. Select the type of water waste from the list, type in the address, and click send.

Many local water agencies have their methods for reporting water waste, whether by email, phone, or online. Southern Nevada Water Crisis, developed by the volunteers, complements those efforts by providing an easy-to-use tool that directly reports water waste to the proper authority in Southern Nevada.

Please leave a name and email address to receive confirmation, and a status update on the report from the local water agency to follow up.

Reporting water waste is only one of the ways you can save water. To learn other easy ways you can conserve water in your community, please visit the Southern Nevada Water Crisis website or send us an email at snwc@gmail.com.

Thank you for helping Southern Nevada save water!
